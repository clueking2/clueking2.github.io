What You Get With Registration:

In addition to a beautiful, well-marked race course through the San Juan Mountains, racers get a finisher shirt, post-race meal, a medal, and lots of photo opportunities.

Race Course:

This course takes you ~26.2 miles through the San Juan Mountain Range of Colorado, topping out at 11,500 feet. It is 100% on trails and is a 2 loop course. The trail you will be the Four Mile Falls/Anderson Trail Loop. More information on the trail can be found by searching the trail on Trail Run Project (app or website).
